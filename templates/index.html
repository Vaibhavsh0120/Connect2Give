Fighting Hunger, Reducing Waste

Every day, tons of perfectly good food goes to waste while millions go hungry. Connect2Give bridges this gap by creating seamless connections between those who have surplus and those who need it most.

Our platform empowers restaurants to make a difference, enables NGOs to serve more people, and gives volunteers a meaningful way to contribute to their community.
